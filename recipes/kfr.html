Description

Kimchi fried rice is fairly easy to explain. It's a traditional fried rice, mixed with kimchi. It's a pretty simple recipe that can be dressed up with whatever the cook desires, such as meats, tofu, fried egg on top, whatever you'd like.

Ingredients

  • 3 Bowls Steamed Rice(3 cups)
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2-3 tablespoons gochujang
  • 3 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 sheet of gim, roasted and shredded into seaweed strips

Steps

  1. Heat up a wide, flat pan or skillet (wok if you have one). Add the vegetable oil
  2. Add the kimchi and stir fry for 1 minute over medium-high heat
  3. Add rice, kimchi juice, water, gochujang. Stir all the ingredients together for about 7 minutes
  4. Add sesame oil and remove from heat
  5. Garnish with sprinkled chopped onion, roasted gim, and sesame seeds, add optional food items on top. Serve immediately.
